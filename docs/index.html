The Ebola Epidemic

The ebola virus is a deadly disease that spreads through contact between infected humans and animals.

Frequency of new cases of Ebola peaked in late 2014, and have been declining since due to improved outbreak control.

Examine the demographics of confirmed ebola cases in countries with intense transmission between 2014-2016 by using the filters and/or by hovering over the data.
